About VIInfotech Careers

VIInfotech Careers is a premier IT training institute located in the heart of BTM 1st Stage, Bengaluru. We specialize in providing comprehensive technology education through both online and offline modes, ensuring flexibility and accessibility for all our students.

Our expert trainers bring years of industry experience and are committed to delivering high-quality education that meets current industry standards. We focus on practical, hands-on learning that prepares our students for real-world challenges in the technology sector.

With a strong emphasis on placement assistance and career guidance, we have successfully helped hundreds of students launch their careers in top IT companies across India.
